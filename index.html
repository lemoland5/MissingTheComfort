Missing the Comfort

I've always been a huge rock fan - especially "filthy" sounds such as grunge guitar and industrial.

Recently, I've been listening to quite a lot of Pearl Jam and Nine Inch Nails. My other favorite rock bands include the likes of Nirvana, Foo Fighters and Weezer. There's something incredibly captivating in a simple, yet catchy riff - something that can satisfy the rock 'itch'.
